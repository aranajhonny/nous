For Nav:
			add left for left menu		 
       		<nav class="sidenav left" role="navigation"></nav>
       		or for right
       		<nav class="sidenav right" role="navigation"></nav>
       		or for top
       		<nav class="sidenav top" role="navigation"></nav>
       		or for top fixed
       		<nav class="sidenav topf" role="navigation"></nav>
       		
Include overlay and controlshint for the menu:
			<div class="overlay"></div>
			<div class="controlshint"><img src="assets/img/swipe.png" alt="Menu Help"></div>

			<section class="wrap">
			
			</section>
